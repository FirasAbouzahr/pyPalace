### Example 00 (Meshing and simulating a Quantum Metal design with pyPalace)

Example 00 is consolidated into [example00_notebook.ipynb](example00_notebook.ipynb). This notebook illustrates a simple example of the Quantum Metal to pyPalace workflow. This includes:
* Constructing a Quantum Metal design,
* Meshing the dessign with pyPalace,
* Building and running the Palace simulation with pyPalace,
* Extracting and analyzing the results with pyPalace.
